One idea a morning, chosen to fall outside the day job (theoretical physics,
machine learning, mathematics). History, craft, cooking, law, folklore,
theology, the biology of strange organisms, economic oddities, whatever
maximises surprise. Each is a short essay that leads with a tension and leaves
a thread hanging rather than tying a bow.

Drafts are written automatically each morning, then reviewed before anything
is published here.
